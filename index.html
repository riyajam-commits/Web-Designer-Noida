Common Questions

Aksar Puche Jaane Waale Sawaal

Website banwane mein kitna time lagta hai?

Static website 3–5 working days mein ready ho jaati hai. Dynamic website 7–14 days mein. Hum deadline ka pakka commitment dete hain aur uspe chalte hain.

Kya domain aur hosting bhi aap doge?

Haan! Standard aur Premium plan mein 1 saal free domain (.com / .in / .org) aur cloud hosting included hai. Basic plan mein alag se le sakte ho — hum guide karenge.

Payment kaise karni hai? Kya advance dena padega?

50% advance mein kaam shuru hota hai, 50% website delivery ke baad. UPI, bank transfer sab accepted hai. Koi pressure nahi — pehle design dekho, pasand aaye toh hi pay karo.

Website banana ke baad kuch change karna ho toh?

1 saal tak free support milti hai. Chhote changes (text, photo) bilkul free. Bade changes (new pages, features) nominal cost pe. WhatsApp karo — hum turant respond karte hain.

Kya aap Noida se bahar bhi kaam karte ho?

Bilkul! Hum India mein sabke liye kaam karte hain — Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, aur baaki sab jagah. International clients bhi hain. Sab kuch online ho jaata hai.

GST invoice milegi kya?

Haan, bilkul. Hum ek registered business hain aur proper GST invoice provide karte hain. Aapke accounts ke liye bilkul sahi rahega.
